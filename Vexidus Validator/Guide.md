## Welcome to Vexidus Validator Guide ##


## Getting Started ##

Our blockchain is open to validators of various types to make a more inclusive community of validators and operators.
Please be aware that becoming a validator does not entitle validators to use or abuse of the blockchain system and
its components. To protect our users and community, validators are expected ot adhere to standards of care and use
in order to be eligible for rewards distributions.

## Requirements ##

- Any home computer with stable internet connection
- VPS including cloud or dedicated, HOWEVER, your provider must allow blockchain activity (See list below)
- Raspberry Pi (4 or 5 recommended with at least 8GB)
- Stake of 10,000 VXS
